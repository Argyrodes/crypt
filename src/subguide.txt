Submission Guide

Basic guide:
1. Fork repo by clicking the fork button in the top right.
2. pull repo to your computer
3. Add writings to 'clib' folder
4.update "clib/yourdoc" to the right filename and 'Title' to the name of your doc.
5. Push to your forked repo then click the Contribute button beneath the repo settings.
6. Put your desired pen name or discord tag in the pull request information and what you are submitting.
7.all submissions will be on the site with 4-6 hours.
